# Privacy Policy for VoIP Phone

**Effective date:** December 23, 2025

VoIP Phone respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the VoIP Phone mobile application.

## Information We Collect

- Device information such as device model, operating system, and IP address
- Call-related metadata such as call duration and connection status (not call content)
- Microphone access for voice calls (only with your permission)
- App usage and crash data for performance improvement

## How We Use Information

- To provide and maintain VoIP calling services
- To improve app performance and reliability
- To fix bugs and respond to support requests
- To comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. Data may be shared only with trusted service providers or when required by law.
